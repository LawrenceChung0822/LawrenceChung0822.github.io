About

我目前是小米的本地化项目经理，主要负责智能手机、IoT 设备及小米汽车的 UI 本地化，覆盖 80+ 语言市场。工作范围主要包括三个部分：中英翻译（英文作为多语言的源语言），项目管理（小米EV），AI Native 探索。

我于 2024 年 5 月进入本地化行业，最初从事游戏本地化，之后转向视频本地化（短剧）。2025 年 7 月，我加入小米，负责全球范围内的软硬件本地化工作。不到一年时间，我从入门到独立负责小米汽车这样的高复杂度本地化项目。这段经历让我相信：成长源于勇于挑战复杂，而非等待万事俱备。

我现在最关注的方向是探索 AI Native 的本地化工作范式，并且正在小米内部的本地化平台 Collie 上搭建 AI Agent，从流程自动化、质量保障、跨语言项目管理等维度，重新思考本地化的工作方式。

我觉得人也应该像产品一样——快速交付，持续迭代。

I'm a Localization Project Manager at Xiaomi, where I lead UI localization for smartphones, IoT devices, and Xiaomi EV across 80+ languages.

I entered the localization industry in May 2024 — starting with game localization, then moving into video localization (short dramas). In July 2025, I joined Xiaomi to take on software and hardware localization at a global scale. Within a year, I went from learning the basics to managing full-scale product localization at one of the world's largest tech companies. This pace taught me that growth comes from jumping into complexity, not waiting until you're ready.

Now, I'm exploring what an AI-native localization workflow looks like. I'm building AI Agents on Collie, Xiaomi's in-house localization management platform, rethinking how we approach process automation, quality assurance, and cross-language project management from the ground up.

I believe in treating yourself as a product — ship fast, iterate often.
